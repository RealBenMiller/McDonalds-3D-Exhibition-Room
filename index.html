Welcome

To demonstrate our capabilities for creating an online, 3D, interactive, virtual public exhibition, Lexington Communications has prepared a mock-up of a hall that could be used for a McDonald's consultation

It features a playable video, interactive board content that can be accessed by clicking the 'info' buttons and more. Every element, from the floor, to the room, to the people attending can be customised to fit your exact preferences.

Lexington's in-house team of designers and web developers create these bespoke rooms to meet your specifications. They are designed to work on all browsers and devices meaning our consultations are inclusive and robust.

If you'd like to learn more, you can contact Lexington North's Digital Lead, Ben Miller at ben.miller@lexcomm.co.uk
